## Ayurvedic Approach to Infertility

In Ayurveda, infertility is known as Vandhyatva. Conception requires the health of four factors — Ritu (proper timing), Kshetra (healthy uterus/reproductive organs), Ambu (proper nourishment), and Beeja (healthy ovum and sperm). Imbalance in any of these factors, often driven by Vata dosha vitiation, Ama accumulation, or Dhatu (tissue) weakness, can lead to difficulty conceiving.

## How We Treat Infertility

At Vaidya Vrindavanam, our approach addresses infertility holistically:

1. **Detoxification** — Panchakarma to cleanse the reproductive channels and remove toxins
2. **Vasthi therapy** — The primary treatment for reproductive health, nourishing the reproductive tissues
3. **Stress management** — Shirodhara and relaxation therapies, as stress is a major factor in infertility
4. **Rejuvenation** — Rasayana (rejuvenation) medicines to strengthen Shukra Dhatu (reproductive tissue)
5. **Lifestyle guidance** — Diet, timing, and lifestyle modifications to support conception
