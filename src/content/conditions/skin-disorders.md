## Ayurvedic Approach to Skin Disorders

In Ayurveda, skin diseases are collectively known as Kushtha — arising from the vitiation of all three doshas, with Pitta and Rakta (blood tissue) playing the primary role. Impure blood, accumulated toxins, and impaired digestion are considered the root causes of most chronic skin conditions.

## How We Treat Skin Disorders

At Vaidya Vrindavanam, our approach includes:

1. **Blood purification** — Raktamoksham (therapeutic bloodletting) to remove impure blood and Pitta toxins
2. **Kapha elimination** — Vamanam to clear systemic toxins contributing to skin eruptions
3. **External nourishment** — Njavara Theppu and medicated oil applications to heal and rejuvenate affected skin
4. **Internal medicines** — Blood-purifying and liver-supporting herbal formulations
5. **Diet & lifestyle** — Pitta-pacifying diet avoiding spicy, sour, and fermented foods

## Recommended Treatments

- **Raktamoksham** — Therapeutic bloodletting for blood purification
- **Vamanam** — Therapeutic emesis to eliminate deep-seated toxins
- **Njavara Theppu** — Medicated rice paste application for skin nourishment
- **Abhyangam & Swedam** — Oil massage with steam for improved skin health

## Duration

Skin conditions typically require longer treatment — 14–28 days is recommended. Chronic conditions like psoriasis may benefit from repeated courses at seasonal intervals.
