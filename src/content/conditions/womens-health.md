## Ayurvedic Approach to Women's Health

Stree Roga (gynaecological disorders) is a major branch of Ayurveda. Women's health conditions are understood through the lens of dosha imbalance, particularly the interplay of Vata (which governs menstruation and reproductive cycles), Pitta (which governs hormones and metabolism), and Kapha (which governs nourishment and tissue growth).

## How We Treat Women's Health Conditions

At Vaidya Vrindavanam, Dr. Ganga SS brings special expertise in Stree Roga. Our approach includes:

1. **Hormonal balance** — Vasthi and internal medicines to regulate hormonal cycles naturally
2. **Stress management** — Shirodhara and relaxation therapies to address the stress-hormone connection
3. **Metabolic support** — Udwarthanam and dietary guidance for conditions like PCOS with weight gain
4. **Nourishment** — Abhyangam and rejuvenation therapies to support reproductive health
5. **Lifestyle guidance** — Personalised diet, exercise, and daily routine recommendations

## Recommended Treatments

- **Vasthi** — Medicated enema therapy to regulate Vata and hormonal balance
- **Abhyangam & Swedam** — Oil massage with steam for overall wellness and circulation
- **Shirodhara** — Oil pouring therapy for stress and hormonal regulation
- **Udwarthanam** — Herbal powder massage for PCOS-related weight management

## Duration

A treatment course of 14–21 days is recommended. Many women's health conditions respond well to cyclical treatment — brief courses aligned with the menstrual cycle over 3–6 months.
