## Ayurvedic Approach to Arthritis

In Ayurveda, arthritis is primarily understood as a Vata disorder — known as Sandhivata (osteoarthritis) or Amavata (rheumatoid arthritis). When Vata dosha becomes aggravated and Ama (toxins) accumulate in the joints, it leads to pain, swelling, stiffness, and restricted movement.

4. **Internal medicines** — Customised Ayurvedic medications to address the root cause
