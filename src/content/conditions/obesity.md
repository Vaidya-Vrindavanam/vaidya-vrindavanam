## Ayurvedic Approach to Obesity

In Ayurveda, obesity is known as Sthoulya — a condition primarily caused by the aggravation of Kapha dosha and Meda Dhatu (fat tissue). When Agni (digestive fire) is weakened, food is not properly metabolised, leading to excessive accumulation of Meda Dhatu while other tissues remain undernourished.
