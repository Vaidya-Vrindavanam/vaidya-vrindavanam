## Ayurvedic Approach to Geriatric Care

Jara Chikitsa (geriatric care) and Rasayana (rejuvenation therapy) are dedicated branches of Ayurveda focused on healthy ageing. As the body ages, Vata dosha naturally increases — leading to dryness, tissue degeneration, weakened immunity, reduced mobility, and cognitive decline. Rasayana therapy counteracts these effects by deeply nourishing all body tissues.

## How We Treat Elderly Patients

At Vaidya Vrindavanam, our geriatric care programme focuses on:

1. **Deep nourishment** — Njavara Kizhi and Njavara Theppu to strengthen muscles, nourish tissues, and improve vitality
2. **Joint and muscle care** — Pizhichil and Abhyangam to lubricate joints, reduce stiffness, and maintain mobility
3. **Mental wellness** — Shirodhara to improve sleep, memory, and mental clarity
4. **Immunity support** — Rasayana herbs to boost immunity and prevent age-related diseases
5. **Gentle approach** — All treatments are adapted in intensity for elderly patients

## Recommended Treatments

- **Njavara Kizhi** — Rice bolus therapy for deep nourishment and muscle strengthening
- **Pizhichil** — Warm oil bath for joint lubrication and full-body rejuvenation
- **Abhyangam & Swedam** — Gentle oil massage with mild steam for overall wellness
- **Shirodhara** — Oil pouring therapy for improved sleep and mental clarity
- **Njavara Theppu** — Rice paste application for skin nourishment and anti-ageing

## Duration

A Rejuvenation Package of 7–14 days is ideal for elderly patients. Regular seasonal courses (2–3 times per year) help maintain health, mobility, and quality of life. All treatments are adjusted in intensity and duration to suit the individual patient's strength and condition.
