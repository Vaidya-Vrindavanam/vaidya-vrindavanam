## Ayurvedic Approach to Stress & Anxiety

In Ayurveda, mental health conditions fall under Mano Vikara — disorders of the mind. Stress and anxiety are primarily caused by the aggravation of Vata dosha in the Manovaha Srotas (channels of the mind). When Prana Vata (the aspect governing mental function) becomes disturbed, it manifests as restlessness, anxiety, fear, insomnia, and inability to concentrate.
