## Ayurvedic Approach to Diabetes

In Ayurveda, diabetes is classified under Prameha — a group of urinary disorders caused by the imbalance of Kapha dosha and impaired Agni (digestive fire). Madhumeha (diabetes mellitus) is considered the most serious form. The condition arises from a sedentary lifestyle, excessive sweet and heavy foods, and metabolic sluggishness.

## How We Treat Diabetes

At Vaidya Vrindavanam, our approach focuses on:

1. **Metabolic correction** — Udwarthanam and herbal therapies to improve metabolism and reduce Kapha
2. **Detoxification** — Vasthi therapy and Panchakarma to cleanse the channels and improve tissue nutrition
3. **External therapies** — Abhyangam to improve circulation, especially to the extremities
4. **Internal medicines** — Time-tested Ayurvedic formulations to support pancreatic function and improve insulin sensitivity
5. **Diet & lifestyle** — Strict Kapha-pacifying dietary plan, exercise regimen, and lifestyle modifications

## Recommended Treatments

- **Udwarthanam** — Herbal powder massage to improve metabolism and reduce excess weight
- **Abhyangam & Swedam** — Oil massage with steam therapy for improved circulation
- **Vasthi** — Medicated enema therapy for deep tissue cleansing and metabolic correction

## Duration

A treatment course of 14–21 days is recommended for comprehensive management. Ayurvedic treatment works as an excellent complement to conventional diabetes care — helping reduce dependency on medications over time with consistent follow-up.
