## 4. Managing Your Content

You have full control over your website content through your **Sanity Studio** content manager. No technical knowledge is required.

**Your Content Manager:** https://vaidya-vrindavanam.sanity.studio

---

### 4.1 Logging In

1. Go to https://vaidya-vrindavanam.sanity.studio
2. Log in with the credentials provided to you separately
3. You will see the dashboard with your content types in the left sidebar: **Treatment**, **Condition**, and **Blog Post**

---

### 4.2 Adding or Editing a Treatment

1. In the left sidebar, click **Treatment**
2. To edit an existing treatment: click its name in the list
3. To add a new treatment: click the **pencil/compose icon** or **"+ New Treatment"** button
4. Fill in the fields:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Name** | The treatment name (e.g., "Abhyangam") |
| **Short Description** | 1–2 sentences shown on the treatments listing page |
| **Category** | Choose: Panchakarma, Massage, or Speciality Treatment |
| **Duration** | How long it takes (e.g., "45 minutes") |
| **Full Description** | Detailed explanation — use the text editor provided |
| **Image** | Upload a photo from your computer (3:2 ratio recommended) |
| **Related Conditions** | Select from your list of conditions — links them together |
| **Featured** | Tick this box if you want it shown on the homepage |
| **Order** | A number that controls the display order (lower = shown first) |

5. Click **Publish** when you are done
6. Your website will automatically update within **1–2 minutes**

---

### 4.3 Adding or Editing a Health Condition

The process is the same as adding a treatment:

1. Click **Condition** in the left sidebar
2. Select an existing condition or create a new one
3. Fill in: Name, Short Description, full content, and the Related Treatments
4. Click **Publish**

---

### 4.4 Publishing a Blog Post

1. Click **Blog Post** in the left sidebar
2. Click the compose/new button to create a new post
3. Fill in the fields:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Title** | The article headline |
| **Excerpt** | A short 1–2 sentence summary (shown on the blog listing page) |
| **Category** | Health Tips, Treatment Guides, Seasonal Advice, or Patient Education |
| **Publish Date** | The date you want shown on the article |
| **Author** | Defaults to Dr. Jayakrishnan T J — you can change this |
| **Content** | The full article — use the rich text editor |

4. Click **Publish**

**Tip:** Publishing 2–3 blog posts per month significantly improves your Google ranking over time.

---

### 4.6 What Happens After You Publish

Every time you click **Publish** in the content manager, the website automatically rebuilds itself and goes live with your changes. This takes **1–2 minutes**. You do not need to call anyone or do anything else — it happens automatically.
